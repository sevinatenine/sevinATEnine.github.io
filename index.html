About

Hi, I’m sevinatenine — a hacker, coder, and tinkerer who loves building projects. I create websites with HTML & React, develop apps, and write Python scripts. I also program in C/C++ and am currently learning Rust.

On the hardware side, I work with Arduino, ESP32, and Raspberry Pi to bring ideas to life. I’m passionate about retro tech and collect classic machines like the Macintosh SE, Commodore 64, and NES.
